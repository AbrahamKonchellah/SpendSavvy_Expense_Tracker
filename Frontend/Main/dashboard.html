How do I add a new transaction?
Go to the Transactions page and click the "Add Transaction" button. Fill in the details and save.
Can I set budgets for different categories?
Yes, go to the Budgets page and click "Set New Budget" to create budgets for different spending categories.
How do I view my spending reports?
Navigate to the Reports page to see various charts and visualizations of your financial data.
Is my financial data secure?
Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect your data and never share it with third parties.
